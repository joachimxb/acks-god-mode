# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in the
ACKS God Mode community a welcoming, harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education,
socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual
identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Accepting responsibility, apologizing to those affected by our mistakes, and
  learning from the experience
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Public or private harassment of any kind
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces — the repository, issues,
pull requests, discussions, and any other official channel — and when an
individual is representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

This is a small, single-maintainer project. Instances of unacceptable behavior may
be reported privately to the maintainer. Because there is no public contact address,
please use GitHub's private channels: open a
[private vulnerability report](https://github.com/joachimxb/acks-god-mode/security/advisories/new)
(it works for conduct reports too) or open a minimal public issue saying only
*"conduct report — please set up a private channel"* with no details, and the
maintainer will follow up privately.

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The maintainer
is obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter of any incident.

## Enforcement Guidelines

The maintainer will follow these community-impact guidelines in determining the
consequences for any action deemed in violation:

1. **Correction** — a private, written warning and clarity about the violation.
2. **Warning** — a warning with consequences for continued behavior; no interaction
   with those involved for a specified period.
3. **Temporary ban** — a temporary ban from any interaction or contribution.
4. **Permanent ban** — a permanent ban from any community interaction.
